How It Works

This simulator uses weighted logical relationships between urban policy decisions and their environmental, social, and economic effects. It does not claim scientific precision — it is designed to make the direction and scale of these relationships visible and discussable.

Policy Main Effect
Public TransportReduces pollution and improves sustainability
Green ZonesImproves quality of life and absorbs CO2
Renewable EnergyCuts emissions; moderate infrastructure cost
RecyclingSupports sustainability; small operational cost
Private CarsRaises pollution, cost and reduces sustainability
